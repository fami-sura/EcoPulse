# 20-minute Intro Call Script (Fiscal Sponsorship)

## Goal

In one call, confirm: **fit**, **timeline**, **fees**, **requirements**, and whether they’ll let you apply to funders under their umbrella.

## 0) Setup (1 min)

- Thanks + confirm time.
- “Goal today: determine if fiscal sponsorship is feasible for EcoPulse in the next 2–4 weeks.”

## 1) The project (3 min)

- EcoPulse: community reporting + verification for waste/drainage/flooding issues.
- Water-first outcomes in Nigeria/Niger/Chad.
- Seeking $50–100k initial grant (AJA Water LOI) to support a 12-month program with an initial 90-day pilot phase.

## 2) What we need from a sponsor (2 min)

- Receive grant funds under 501(c)(3)
- Allow us to apply using sponsor EIN (when appropriate)
- Disburse to vendors/contractors and provide monthly/quarterly reporting
- Handle international operations (vendors in Nigeria/Niger/Chad)

## 3) Sponsor questions (10 min)

### Eligibility + timeline

- Do you sponsor early-stage projects without an existing legal entity?
- What’s your **onboarding timeline** from “yes” to “able to receive funds”?
- Do you require committed funding before onboarding?

### Fee model

- What’s the % fee and are there startup/transfer fees?
- Any monthly minimums?
- Are fees different for Model A vs Model C?

### International operations

- Can you pay vendors/contractors internationally?
- Can you reimburse local expenses on invoices/receipts?
- Any restricted countries/AML constraints relevant to Nigeria/Niger/Chad?

### Compliance + control

- What oversight do you require (approvals, budgets, timesheets)?
- Can EcoPulse remain the program lead (Model A) vs regrant relationship (Model C)?

### Fundraising + applications

- Can we submit the AJA LOI listing your 501(c)(3) and EIN if we’re sponsored?
- If sponsorship isn’t finalized yet, can we state “fiscal sponsor in progress”?

## 4) Close (4 min)

- Ask for next steps: “What do you need from us to proceed?”
- Confirm who owns follow-ups and by when.
- Request a written summary of fees + requirements.

## After-call checklist

- Update `tracking.csv`
- Email sponsor pack + budget one-pager
- Book follow-up (if fit)
