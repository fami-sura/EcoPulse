# EcoPulse — Draft Pilot Budget (12 months)

This is a simple planning budget to share with potential fiscal sponsors. Adjust once a sponsor confirms fee structure and payment mechanics.

## Budget range

- Target ask: **$50,000 – $100,000**
- Example build: **$75,000**

## Draft allocation (example: $75,000)

### Program delivery (field + community)

- Local coordination (part-time coordinators across focus areas): $18,000
- Community verification activities (meetups, stipends, transport): $7,500
- Education/engagement materials (lightweight, mobile-first): $3,000

### Product & data (MVP delivery)

- Engineering / product build support (contract): $18,000
- Hosting + tooling (Vercel, monitoring, email, maps): $2,500
- Data operations / QA (verification workflows): $4,000

### Monitoring, evaluation, learning

- Baseline + endline (simple) + monthly reporting: $5,000

### Operations & compliance

- Legal/financial/admin basics (nonprofit compliance support, accounting): $5,000

### Contingency

- Contingency / FX / unexpected costs (~8%): $6,000

### Fiscal sponsor fee (placeholder)

- Estimated fee (5–12%, depends on sponsor + model): **$6,000**

## Notes for sponsors

- Funds primarily used for program delivery + contractors/vendors.
- No government grants anticipated in this phase.
- Payment mechanics can be tailored: direct vendor payments, reimbursements, or local partner subgrants where compliant.
